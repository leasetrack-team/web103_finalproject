## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

Our group collaborated well. There was a clear separation of responsibilities and everything worked out. We successfully created the entity relationship diagram and designed wireframes for the main pages of the application, including Sign In, My Requests, New Request, Request Details, and Assigned Work. Creating the wireframes helped us visualize how tenants, technicians, and managers will interact with the application. We also communicated our ideas, shared feedback, and worked together to make important design decisions.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

One challenge was defining the database relationships, foreign keys, and the many-to-many relationship to ensure that our ERD was accurate. Another challenge was deciding which pages and information were most important for each user role while creating the wireframes.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

We would appreciate any feedback on our entity relationship diagram and wireframes to make sure that the database structure, relationships, and user flows are clear and correctly designed. Guidance on any changes we should make before starting the implementation would also be very helpful.
